Primary Muscles

  • Glute
  • Quad
  • Thigh - Inner

Secondary Muscles

  • Hamstring

Steps

  1. Adjust the seat of the leg press machine so that your knees are at a ~90-degree angle when your feet are on the footplate.
  2. Sit on the machine and place your feet shoulder-width apart on the footplate.
  3. Push the footplate away from your body by extending your legs.
  4. Lower the footplate back towards your body by bending your legs and keeping your feet flat on the plate.
  5. Repeat.
