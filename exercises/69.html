  1. Stand with feet hip-width apart and toes pointing forward.
  2. Hold a barbell in front of your thighs with an overhand grip.
  3. Engage your core and keep your back straight as you hinge forward at the hips, pushing your glutes back.
  4. Lower the weight towards the ground, keeping your legs straight but not locked.
  5. Pause when you feel a stretch in your hamstrings, then use your glutes and hamstrings to lift the weight back up to the starting position.
  6. Repeat.
