  • Rotator Cuff - Front
    1. Begin by lying on your side on a flat bench with your arm extended straight out in front of you and your elbow bent at a 90-degree angle.
    2. Hold a dumbbell in your hand with your palm facing up towards the ceiling.
    3. Slowly rotate your arm inwards towards your body, keeping your elbow bent and your upper arm stationary.
    4. Pause for a moment at the top of the movement, then slowly lower the dumbbell back to the starting position.
    5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch sides and repeat the exercise with your other arm.
