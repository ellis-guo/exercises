Deadlift With High Handles – Trap Bar

←Back to Exercise Directory

Primary Muscles

  • Glute
  • Lower Back
  • Quad

Secondary Muscles

  • Forearm - Inner
  • Thigh - Inner
  • Trap

Steps

  1. Begin by standing in the center of the trap bar with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Grab the high handles of the trap bar with an overhand grip.
  3. Brace your core and lift the trap bar by extending your hips and knees.
  4. Keep your back straight and your shoulders pulled back throughout the lift.
  5. Once you reach a standing position, slowly lower the trap bar back down to the ground.
  6. Repeat.
