Pullover — Barbell

←Back to Exercise Directory

Primary Muscles

  • Tricep

Secondary Muscles

    Steps

    1. Start by lying flat on a bench with your feet firmly on the ground and your head at the end of the bench.
    2. Hold a barbell with an overhand grip, with your hands shoulder-width apart and your arms extended straight up at an angle that brings the barbell above your head.
    3. Slowly lower the barbell towards the top of your head by bending your elbows, keeping your upper arms stationary and tension on your triceps.
    4. Pause when the barbell is just above the top of your head, then slowly raise it back to the starting position by extending your arms.
    5. Repeat.
