Horizontal External Shoulder Rotation – Dumbbell

←Back to Exercise Directory

Primary Muscles

  • Rotator Cuff - Back

Steps

  1. Start by lying on your side on a bench or mat, with your arm extended straight out in front of you and holding a dumbbell in your hand.
  2. Keep your elbow bent at a 90-degree angle and your upper arm pressed against your side.
  3. Slowly rotate your arm outwards, away from your body, until your forearm points towards the ceiling.
  4. Hold this position for a few seconds, then slowly lower your arm back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch sides and repeat the exercise with your other arm.
