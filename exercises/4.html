Romanian Deadlift – Barbell

←Back to Exercise Directory

Primary Muscles

  • Glute
  • Hamstring
  • Lower Back

Secondary Muscles

  • Forearm - Inner
  • Thigh - Inner
  • Trap

Steps

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Hold a barbell with an overhand grip in front of your thighs .
  2. Brace your core, keep your chest up, and slowly bend your knees while hinging at your hips. As you hinge, push your hips back and lower the weight toward the floor. Keep your back straight and your shoulders down.
  3. Continue lowering the weight until you feel a stretch in your hamstrings. Your knees should be slightly bent but not locked. You can lower the weight until it touches the floor or stops just below your knees.
  4. Pause briefly at the bottom of the movement, then slowly reverse the motion by driving your hips forward and standing up tall. As you stand up, squeeze your glutes and hamstrings.
  5. Repeat.
