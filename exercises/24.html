Burpee – BOSU

←Back to Exercise Directory

Primary Muscles

  • Chest
  • Quad
  • Shoulder - Front
  • Shoulder - Side

Secondary Muscles

  • Abdominal
  • Trap

Steps

  1. Start in a standing position with the BOSU ball on the ground (inflated-side down) in front of you.
  2. Place your hands on the BOSU ball and jump your feet back into a plank position.
  3. Perform a push-up while keeping your hands on the BOSU ball.
  4. Jump your feet back towards the BOSU ball and stand up.
  5. Lift the BOSU ball overhead and jump up, extending your arms towards the ceiling.
  6. Land softly and repeat the exercise for desired number of reps.
