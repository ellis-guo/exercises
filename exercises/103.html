  1. Begin by lying flat on a bench with your feet firmly planted on the ground.

  3. Slowly lower the dumbbell behind your head, keeping your arms straight and your core engaged.
  4. Pause when the dumbbell is parallel to the ground, then slowly lift it back to the starting position.
