    Steps

    1. Stand on a raised platform or step with the balls of your feet on the edge and your heels hanging off.
    2. Raise your heels up as high as possible.
    3. Shift your weight onto one foot and slowly lower your heel down below the platform.
    4. Use your other foot to push yourself back up to the starting position.
    5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions on one leg before switching to the other.
